# product_context

## Player
Roguelike-literate PC player. Has played Risk of Rain 2, Gunfire Reborn, Deep Rock. Expects:
- Quick session start (lobby → in-run in <90s).
- Builds that visibly change gameplay, not just stat bumps.
- Co-op friends list flow that doesn't require Discord coordination.

## Why they pick voidrun over RoR2
- Top-down readability at 4-player chaos (RoR2 3D gets visually noisy).
- Deterministic seeded runs for daily challenge leaderboards.
- Faster TTK — 30min runs not 60min.

## Monetization
Premium $19.99. One paid expansion planned y+1. No MTX, no battle pass.

## Success metrics
- D1 retention >35%, D7 >15%.
- Median run completion attempt rate >60% (i.e. people finish runs they start).
- <2% sessions hit a desync that ends the run.
